# AI Ethics Documentation

## Overview

The AI HR Interview System has been designed to assist recruiters by providing objective interview analysis while maintaining fairness, transparency, and candidate privacy.

This AI system is intended as a decision-support tool and does not replace human judgment.

---

# Candidate Consent

Before starting an interview, candidates are informed that:

- AI is being used during evaluation.
- Audio responses may be processed for analysis.
- Interview transcripts may be temporarily stored.
- Candidates may withdraw consent before the interview begins.

The Consent Manager verifies candidate approval before evaluation.

---

# Responsible AI Principles

The system follows these principles:

- Fairness
- Transparency
- Privacy
- Explainability
- Human Oversight

---

# Human-in-the-loop

The final hiring decision is always made by a recruiter.

AI-generated scores are recommendations only.

---

# Privacy

The system stores only information required for evaluation.

Personally identifiable information is separated from scoring logic whenever possible.

---

# Data Usage

Interview data is used only for recruitment purposes.

No candidate information is shared with third parties.

---

# Explainability

Every generated score can be explained using:

- Communication analysis
- Confidence analysis
- HR interview evaluation
- Aptitude evaluation

Recruiters receive explanation notes together with candidate scores.

---

# Bias Prevention

The AI system does not intentionally use:

- Age
- Gender
- Religion
- Race
- Nationality
- Marital Status
- Caste
- Disability
- Political Views

Scoring is based only on job-related competencies.

---

# Compliance

The system supports:

- Candidate consent verification
- Explainable AI
- Fair scoring
- Data retention policy
- Human review

The system is designed to align with responsible AI principles and modern recruitment practices.
