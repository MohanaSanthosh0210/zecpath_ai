# Integrity Detection Architecture

Version: 1.0

---

# Objective

Design a non-invasive AI framework capable of identifying possible malpractice
during technical or HR interviews.

The framework assists recruiters by providing integrity-related observations.
It never makes automatic accusations or hiring decisions.

---

# Inputs

Behavioral signals collected during interview sessions.

Possible future sources

- Browser activity
- Window focus
- Camera observations
- Audio observations
- Behavioral AI module

---

# Processing Pipeline

Interview Session

↓

Behavior Signal Collection

↓

Integrity Signal Mapping

↓

Threshold Evaluation

↓

Pattern Recognition

↓

Risk Assessment

↓

Warning Engine

↓

Integrity Report

---

# Core Modules

## Signal Mapper

Maps observed events into predefined integrity signals.

---

## Risk Engine

Converts signal severity into overall interview risk.

---

## Warning Engine

Determines recruiter notifications.

---

## Integrity Designer

Produces the complete framework configuration.

---

## Report Generator

Creates recruiter-readable integrity reports.

---

# Outputs

The framework produces

- Integrity Events
- Risk Level
- Warning Messages
- Integrity Report
