# Question Bank Architecture

## Overview

The Question Bank stores all HR interview questions in a structured format.

Questions are categorized first by interview topic and then filtered according to candidate profile.

---

# Question Categories

## Self Introduction

Example:

- Tell me about yourself.

---

## Career Journey

Example:

- Describe your career journey.

---

## Strengths & Weaknesses

Examples:

- What are your strengths?

- What is your biggest weakness?

---

## Teamwork & Culture Fit

Examples:

- Tell me about a successful team project.

- How do you resolve conflicts?

---

## Career Goals

Example:

- Where do you see yourself in five years?

---

## Availability & Commitment

Examples:

- When can you join?

- Are you willing to relocate?

---

# Role-Based Questions

## Fresher

Examples:

- Explain your final-year project.

- Why should we hire you?

---

## Experienced

Examples:

- Describe your most recent role.

- What is your biggest achievement?

---

## Technical Roles

Examples:

- Explain a challenging technical project.

- How do you improve your technical skills?

---

## Non-Technical Roles

Examples:

- How do you prioritize work?

- Describe a difficult customer interaction.

---
