# Adaptive Questioning Framework

## Overview

The Adaptive Questioning Framework enables the AI interviewer to modify interview difficulty according to candidate performance.

Instead of asking identical questions to every candidate, the AI dynamically changes the interview based on response quality.

---

# Framework Logic

Candidate Response

↓

Evaluate Response Quality

↓

Evaluate Confidence

↓

Determine Difficulty

↓

Generate Best Follow-Up

---

# Difficulty Levels

## Simple

Triggered when:

- Short responses
- Missing details
- Incomplete explanations

Follow-up

- Explain further
- Clarify answer

---

## Medium

Triggered when:

- Reasonable explanation
- Moderate confidence

Follow-up

- Expand answer
- Describe additional experience

---

## Advanced

Triggered when:

- Detailed response
- High confidence
- Strong communication

Follow-up

- Scenario-based questions
- Real-world examples
- Decision-making situations

---

# Adaptation Rules

## Rule 1

Short Answer

↓

Ask Deepening Question

---

## Rule 2

Vague Answer

↓

Ask Clarification Question

---

## Rule 3

Confident Answer

↓

Ask Scenario Question

---

## Rule 4

Repeated Follow-Up

↓

Move to Next Topic
