# Logical Reasoning Scoring Model

## Objective

Measure how effectively the candidate demonstrates logical thinking
during interview responses.

---

# Evaluation Parameters

## Keyword Coverage

Measures how many expected reasoning concepts appear in the answer.

Example

Expected

- communicate
- prioritize
- analyze
- solution

Candidate

"I would analyze the issue and communicate with the team."

Coverage

50%

---

## Scenario Understanding

Checks whether the candidate addresses the core interview scenario.

---

## Problem Solving

Measures whether the candidate explains a structured solution.

Expected sequence

Problem

↓

Analysis

↓

Solution

↓

Outcome

---

## Reasoning Structure

Checks whether the explanation follows a logical flow.

---

# Scoring Formula

Logical Reasoning Score

=

Keyword Coverage × 40%

+

Scenario Evaluation × 30%

+

Problem Solving × 20%

+

Reasoning Structure × 10%

---
