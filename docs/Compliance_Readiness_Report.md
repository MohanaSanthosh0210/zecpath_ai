Assess whether the AI HR Interview System satisfies basic compliance requirements.

---

# Explainability

Recruiters receive explanations for:

- Communication Score
- Confidence Score
- HR Interview Score
- Aptitude Score
- Unified Hiring Score

---

# Data Retention

Interview data should be retained only for the organization's approved retention period.

Candidate records should be removable upon request.

---

# Security

Sensitive interview information should be stored securely.

Access should be limited to authorized personnel.

---

# Human Oversight

AI recommendations are advisory only.

Recruiters retain full authority over hiring decisions.

---

# Overall Compliance Status

READY

The HR Interview AI system satisfies the documented ethical and compliance requirements for responsible AI-assisted recruitment.
