# Malpractice Detection Logic

---

## Objective

Define measurable integrity signals that may indicate possible external
assistance or interview malpractice.

---

## Signals

### Tab Switching

Possible indication of searching for answers.

---

### Screen Focus Loss

Interview window loses focus.

---

### External Voice

Additional speakers detected.

---

### Repeated Gaze Deviation

Candidate frequently looks away.

---

### Long Response Pause

Extended silence before answering.

---

## Evaluation Strategy

Each signal

↓

Threshold Evaluation

↓

Pattern Recognition

↓

Risk Score

↓

Recruiter Warning

---

## Important Note

Individual signals alone do not indicate malpractice.

Only repeated patterns contribute to elevated integrity risk.
