# Component Description

## Resume Parser

Extracts structured information from candidate resumes.

---

## ATS Engine

Calculates resume compatibility with the job description.

---

## Speech Processing

Converts interview audio into machine-readable text.

---

## Transcript Cleaning

Removes filler words and standardizes transcripts.

---

## Answer Understanding

Identifies candidate intent and extracts important entities.

---

## Screening Scoring

Calculates objective screening scores using multiple evaluation metrics.

---

## Behavior Analysis

Measures candidate confidence, sentiment, and communication quality.

---

## Conversation Flow

Controls interview progression, retries, and follow-up questions.

---

## Testing & Optimization

Improves system performance through testing and threshold optimization.

---

## Edge Case Handling

Handles poor audio, language mixing, missing responses, and background noise.

---

## Final AI Evaluation

Aggregates all AI module outputs into a recruiter-ready hiring recommendation.
