# Scenario Evaluation Framework

## Objective

Evaluate candidate performance in situational judgment interview
questions.

---

# Evaluation Workflow

Interview Question

↓

Candidate Response

↓

Speech-to-Text

↓

Answer Understanding

↓

Scenario Evaluation

↓

Aptitude Report

---

# Evaluation Criteria

## Situation Understanding

Does the candidate understand the problem?

---

## Decision Quality

Is the proposed decision reasonable?

---

## Communication

Is the explanation clear?

---

## Problem Solving

Does the answer include a practical solution?

---

## Completeness

Does the candidate cover all important aspects?

---

# Evaluation Metrics

Situation Understanding

0–100

Decision Quality

0–100

Problem Solving

0–100

Communication

0–100

---

# Final Scenario Score

Average of

- Situation Understanding
- Decision Quality
- Problem Solving
- Communication
